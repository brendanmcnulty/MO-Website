Beautifying New York One Garden At A Time

McNulty Outdoors Inc. is a full service landscaping company working throughout Manhattan, Queens, Brooklyn, and Western Nassau County. Our staff can provide anything from one flat of flowers to an entire new landscape. Whether it’s working through a brownstone, a courtyard, a private yard, public garden or the rooftop terrace of a Manhattan high rise, we have the expertise to complete your project both safely and properly. We specialize in directed, personalized service, tailored to each client’s individual needs. Whether you need to revamp your garden on Long Island, or want maintenance of your terrace planters in the city, we can individualize a plan that meets your needs and surpasses your expectations.
